About the University

The Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB) is one of Nigeria’s leading institutions in agricultural education, research, and innovation. Established in 1988, FUNAAB focuses on producing graduates who are capable of transforming Nigeria’s agricultural sector through scientific and technological advancement.
